Intresting Mexico Facts

Mexican Silver Pesos Were The First Global Currency

Using silver mined in Mexico, the colonial government minted a zillion of these silver coins. They ere used in Spain, the Caribbean, and Southeast Asia. In fact, these silver coins- also known as Spanish dollars - are famous "Pieces of Eight" you hear in pirate stories. They were called pieces of eight because they were worth wight reals - the currency of Mexico.

Mexico Is Home To One Of The Six Cradles Of Civilization

Human civilization in Mexico is thought to date back to at least 8,000 BC. This is where you will find the Olmec, Toltec, Teotihuacan, Zapotec, Mayan, and Aztec empires. This puts Mexico up there with the other so - called cradles - Ancient India, Egypt, China, Peru, Mesopotamia.

Mexico Is A Mixed Pot

Mexico was a colony of Spain for over 300 years. Over that lengthy period, not only Spanish, but other Europeans - as well as Afican slaves - joined the popuation of already different native ethnic groups.
